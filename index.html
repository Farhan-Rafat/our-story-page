Our Story

How Skylark came to be

kayne brennan

technical director

30th a pril 2023

Skylark is an agency with a long history in digital design.

Established in 2009 by Arthur Irving, a BAFTA award winning designer with a background in building digital web experiences for the world of TV, Arthur began his own agency so he could truly be free of the constraints of working for a big corporate stifling his creative and innovative ways.

“Working in TV became a living hell. Just trying to get the simplest thing done was a headache, and I felt that if I truly wanted to explore innovation and push the boundaries then I needed to rip the plaster off, remove the comfort of a nine to five and go it alone”

arthur irving

creative director

Skylark rapidly grew to a small team based out of Kingsland Road in Dalston, where they made a reputation for themselves as a team of digital innovators, being invited to conferences to present their work, and being called upon in the digital worlds of TV and entertainment to create new digital brand experiences and websites. Over the years Skylark partnered with a number of different development agencies, one of which in more recent times was owned by his now business partners Kayne and Ben.

team of the years

Although Ben and Arthur go way back to their days of playing semi-professional rugby, it wasn't until 2019 that there was an opportunity for both companies to work together. A new client had been introduced to Kayne and Ben, who at the time were not being happy with their current design partners, thinking they had something that was worthy of putting under Arthur's nose, the team came together to build a pretty cool AI product for a client, and their working relationship began.

In 2020, the world as we knew it took a bizarre turn upside down in the form of a global pandemic.

Having recently started sharing an office to fulfil another project together, a simple conversation centred around the future is now uncertain and we are stronger together pushed the three to create a new beginning and Skylark Digital was born.

recent teams

In under 3 years the team have grown from a combined team of 7 to an international team of 22 plus partners we work with on a daily basis.

Our client base has grown and we have worked on projects for DreamWorks Animations, Arsenal football club, the European Hockey Federation and more, with many more on the horizon for the coming years.

Today Skylark has the ambition and drive to become a leading digital transformation agency.

We are passionate about all things digital and have built a team and culture we are just so proud of. Our people make us who we are, the three founders care deeply about them and although we are all digital nomads most of the time, we have fostered a communication and togetherness that everyone enjoys and helps us work together.
